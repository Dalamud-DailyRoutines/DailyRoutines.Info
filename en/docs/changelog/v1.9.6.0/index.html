v1.9.6.0 Update Notes

Modifications on Daily Routines

Section titled “Modifications on Daily Routines”
  • Preview images for modules were introduced. Modules with available preview images would have a Preview Image tag in its tag area. Hovering the cursor for detail.
  • The drawing logic of many elements in several internal interfaces was optimized.
  • New module category Recruitment, for modules which are related to Party Finder and Duty Finder.
    • Of course some modules were moved to the Recruitment category.

New modules

Section titled “New modules”

Auto Adjust Battle Effect Visibility [Combat]

Section titled “Auto Adjust Battle Effect Visibility [Combat]”

Recommended Module

Automatically adjust the visibility settings of battle effects based on the area and the number of players nearby after proper configurations.

Frontline Map Radar [Combat]

Section titled “Frontline Map Radar [Combat]”

Automatically provide key information on the map while in Frontline, including health & location of loaded players nearby and countdown & tactical point of objectives.
